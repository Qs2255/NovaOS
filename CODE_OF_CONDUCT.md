## 1. Core Values

- Respect others regardless of background or skill level.  
- Assume good intentions and act in good faith.  
- Keep conversations focused on collaboration and problem-solving.  
- Encourage learning, patience, and mentorship.

---

## 2. Expected Behavior

- Use inclusive and professional language.  
- Welcome new contributors kindly.  
- Respect differing opinions and experiences.  
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.  
- Support community decisions and processes.

---

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.  
- Offensive comments or personal attacks.  
- Trolling, spam, or irrelevant self-promotion.  
- Sharing private information without consent.  
- Intentionally spreading misinformation about the project.

---

## 4. Enforcement

- Violations may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent exclusion.  
- Reports of misconduct can be sent privately to: **conduct@novaos.org** (or repo maintainer).  
- Decisions are made transparently by maintainers and community moderators.

---

## 5. Acknowledgment

Based on the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).  
All participants agree to uphold this code as a condition of contributing.
